My name is Hojin, and I am a student at Georgia Tech. I am focused on bridging soft matter science with pressing biomedical needs through our flagship hydro-softened materials. Hydro-softening is bio-inspired mechanical softening effect driven by the spatial organization of water within free volume domains, enabled by steric confinement, directional structuring, and periodic functionalization.
